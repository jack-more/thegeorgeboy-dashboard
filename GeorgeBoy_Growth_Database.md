## GROWTH STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: Feature Page Submissions
**Goal:** Get featured on major dog pages

**Action Steps:**
1. [ ] Create 3-5 "feature-worthy" posts (high quality, good lighting, engaging caption)
2. [ ] Submit to all Tier 1 pages in one week
3. [ ] Tag appropriate accounts in every new post
4. [ ] Follow up via DM after 1 week if no response

### Strategy 2: Community Engagement (The 10-10-10 Method)
**Goal:** Build genuine relationships with other dog accounts

**Daily Tasks:**
- [ ] Comment meaningfully on 10 posts from mid-tier accounts
- [ ] Like 10 recent posts from accounts in your niche
- [ ] Respond to 10 comments/DMs on your own posts

**Weekly Tasks:**
- [ ] Engage with 5 new accounts in 15k-150k range
- [ ] Share/repost 1-2 posts from community members (with credit)

### Strategy 3: Content Pillars for George

**Pillar 1: The Unique Look**
- "What breed is he?" posts (people love guessing)
- DNA test reveal content
- Comparison photos with Brussels Griffons/Affenpinschers

**Pillar 2: Personality Content**
- Funny faces and expressions
- George's quirks and habits
- Day in the life content

**Pillar 3: Relatable Dog Parent Content**
- Morning routines
- Treat time
- Walk adventures

**Pillar 4: Trending/Viral Formats**
- Audio trends adapted for George
- "POV" content
- Before/after grooming

### Strategy 4: Engagement Pod Participation
**Goal:** Find and join legitimate engagement groups

**Where to Find:**
- Reddit: r/InstagramMarketing, r/SocialMediaMarketing
- Facebook Groups: "Instagram Engagement Pods"
- Discord servers (see section below)

**Rules for Pod Participation:**
- Only join pods with similar follower counts
- Engage authentically, not just "nice!"
- Be consistent - if you join, participate daily

### Strategy 5: Collaboration & Cross-Promotion
**Goal:** Partner with similar-sized accounts

**Ideas:**
- Joint giveaways with other small dog accounts
- "Takeover" each other's stories
- Create duet/collab content
- Feature each other in posts

## TIKTOK STRATEGY

### Why TikTok Matters for George:
- **Faster growth potential** than Instagram (algorithm favors new creators)
- Pet content performs extremely well on TikTok
- Can drive followers TO Instagram
- One viral TikTok can explode your entire presence

### TikTok Content That Goes Viral:
| Content Type | Example for George |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Funny Reactions | George's face when he hears "bath" or "vet" |
| POV Videos | "POV: You're a treat and I'm staring at you" |
| Pet Fails | George being dramatically clumsy |
| Before/After | Grooming transformations |
| Training Tricks | Teaching George something new |
| Zoomies | George's wild energy moments |
| Sneaky Behavior | George stealing food/toys |
| Emotional Content | Rescue story, bond moments |

### TikTok Technical Tips:
- **Video Length:** 7-15 seconds for highest retention
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds MUST grab attention (funny face, surprising moment)
- **Filming:** At George's eye level for engaging shots
- **Trending Audio:** USE IT! Check FYP for popular sounds
- **Posting Time:** Test different times, but evenings often work well

### TikTok Hashtag Strategy:
**Primary Tags:**
- #DogsOfTikTok #DogTikTok #PetTok #FunnyDogs
- #DogLover #PetLife #ViralPet #FurryFriends

**Niche Tags (for George's look):**
- #WeirdDog #UglyDog #FunnyFace #MixedBreed
- #SmallDog #ScrufffyDog #MonkeyDog

**Trending Challenge Tags:**
- #ClapForYourDog #ObstacleChallenge
- Check trending page daily for new challenges!

### TikTok Growth Strategy:
1. **Post 1-3x daily** (TikTok rewards frequency more than Instagram)
2. **Jump on trends FAST** (within 24-48 hours of trend emerging)
3. **Cross-post to Instagram Reels** (repurpose content!)
4. **Engage in comments** on other pet videos
5. **Duet/Stitch** popular pet content
6. **Go Live** occasionally to boost algorithm favor

### TikTok Viral Content Ideas for George:
1. "What breed is this?" - Show George, let people guess (engagement bait!)
2. "My dog looks like [celebrity/character]" - George's unique face
3. "Dogs with human expressions" trend
4. "Tell me you have a [mixed breed] without telling me"
5. "Rating my dog's dramatic reactions"
6. "Things my dog does that make no sense"

### TikTok vs Instagram Strategy:
| Platform | Best For | Post Frequency | Content Style |
|----------|----------|----------------|---------------|
| TikTok | Viral growth, discovery | 1-3x daily | Raw, funny, trendy |
| Instagram | Community, monetization | 1x daily | Polished, aesthetic |
| **Strategy:** Create on TikTok → Repurpose best performers to Instagram Reels |

### TikTok Monetization (Future):
- Creator Fund (1K+ followers)
- Brand deals (often start smaller than Instagram)
- TikTok Shop (pet products)
- Drive traffic to Instagram for bigger brand deals
